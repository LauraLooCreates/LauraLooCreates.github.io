Definition:

Fiora: In Irish and Scottish Gaelic contexts, "fíor" (or fìor in Scottish) means "true," "genuine," "real," or "pure". It is often used as a prefix to mean "very" or "extreme".
As a name, "Fíora" is sometimes interpreted as an Irish word for "true" or as a feminine variant of fionn (fair/blessed)
